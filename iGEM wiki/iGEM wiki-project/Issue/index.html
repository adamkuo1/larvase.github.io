Issue

Technology has significantly changed the way people communicate in recent years. With the development of smartphones and social media platforms, individuals can now connect instantly with others around the world. This convenience has improved both personal and professional communication. For instance, students can easily form online study groups, and businesses can hold virtual meetings with international partners. Although some argue that face-to-face interaction is decreasing, the overall impact of modern communication technology has been largely positive.
